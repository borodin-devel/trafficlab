# Spectral-Density Software Architecture Document

## Context and Status

Goal is future comparison of periodic and frequency structure. Architecture
does not define regularized traffic series, sample interval, horizon,
detrending, taper, periodogram/Welch estimator, grid alignment, spectral
distance, normalization, score, or resource bounds. Selection remains
unsupported. Research must address leakage, aliasing, zero power, and short records.
