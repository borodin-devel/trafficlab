# Sequence-Kernel MMD Configuration

Required finite positive settings are horizon, fixed window width, IAT and
frame-length scales, Gaussian-RBF bandwidth, score mapping scale, and numerical
absolute tolerance. Signature maximum degree is positive integer; every degree
`0..maximum` has a positive exact weight. Exact TOML paths/defaults, maximum
windows/path length/degree, library/backend, and deterministic controls are
unresolved and must be approved before implementation.
